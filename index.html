How It Works

A simple, streamlined workflow
to manage your academic journey

01
Register & Setup
Students and supervisors register on the platform and complete their profiles with relevant academic information, programme details, and research topics.
02
Define Milestones
Set academic milestones, deadlines, and assessment criteria tailored to your programme requirements across all 8 pipeline stages.
03
Track Progress
Monitor progress through interactive dashboards, submit work, and receive feedback in real-time. Present at seminars and advance through gated stages.
04
Review & Assess
Supervisors review submissions, provide structured feedback, and assess student performance. Corrections are AI-extracted and validated by the chair.
